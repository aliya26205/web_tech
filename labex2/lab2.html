Self-confidence
is the belief in one's own abilities and worth, which helps in facing challenges and achieving goals. It fosters a positive outlook, allows for taking risks, and provides the courage to pursue dreams and communicate assertively.

Self-discipline
is the ability to control one's behavior, emotions, and thoughts to achieve long-term goals, and it is crucial for success in life. It involves consistently staying focused, managing time effectively, and resisting distractions and impulses to build good habits and stay on track, even when motivation is low.

Self-control
is the ability to regulate your emotions, impulses, and behaviors to achieve long-term goals. It involves resisting immediate temptations and making choices that are beneficial in the long run, which can lead to greater success, stronger character, and improved well-being.
